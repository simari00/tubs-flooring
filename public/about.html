The Tubs Standard

Founded on a commitment to uncompromising quality, Tubs Flooring specializes in the precise sourcing and installation of natural hardwood and premium synthetic surfaces.

With our logistical hub anchored securely in the Western Cape of South Africa, we execute high-end architectural flooring projects for commercial developers and luxury residential clients globally.

We do not just lay floors; we engineer architectural foundations, ensuring every single plank meets our exacting international standards for durability, aesthetic, and environmental compliance.
