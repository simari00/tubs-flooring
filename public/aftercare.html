Protect Your Investment

Premium floors demand premium care. Follow our expert guidelines to ensure your engineered wood and synthetic surfaces maintain their flawless aesthetic for decades.

Control Humidity

Natural wood breathes. Keep indoor humidity between 40-60% to prevent the expansion and contraction that leads to warping.

Immediate Cleanup

Spills are inevitable, but stains are preventable. Wipe up liquids immediately using a dry or slightly damp microfibre cloth.

Use Right Products

Never use harsh chemicals, wax, or steam cleaners. Only use pH-neutral cleaners specifically formulated for your material.
